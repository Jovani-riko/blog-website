Hardware Maintenance Tips

Regular hardware maintenance keeps your computer running efficiently. Clean out dust, ensure fans work, and consider reapplying CPU thermal paste. Check hard drives for errors, defragment regularly, secure all cables, use surge protectors, and inspect batteries and hardware components for damage.

  • Keep your computer clean and dust-free

  • Laptop Hinges

    • Laptop hinges are essential but are among the most frequently and easily damaged parts. Rough handling, side-opening, or dirt can cause them to loosen or break, potentially harming the screen or internal cables. Open the lid gently from the center and keep hinges clean to extend their lifespan.

Software Maintenance Tips

  • Regularly update your operating system and software
  • Uninstall unused programs to free up space.
  • use disk cleanup tools to remove junk files.
  • Defragment your hard drive (if using HDD).

Security Tips

  • Install a reliable antivirus program.
  • Use strong, unique passwords.
  • Back up your data regularly.
  • Be cautious when opening email attachments or clicking links.
